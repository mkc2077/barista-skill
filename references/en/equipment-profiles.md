| Comandante | C40 MK3 / MK4 | Pour-over / espresso | 40 clicks, benchmark standard |

**Hand grinder espresso starting clicks** (reference only, calibrate yourself):
- 1Zpresso JX-Pro: 1.0–1.2 turns
- 1Zpresso K-Plus: 1.0–1.5 turns
- Comandante C40: 14–16 clicks
- Timemore C2: 8–10 clicks

**Hand grinder pour-over starting clicks**:
- Comandante C40: 22–26 clicks
- 1Zpresso JX: 2.0–2.4 turns
- Timemore Chestnut X: 14–18 clicks

## 5. When user information is insufficient

- "Just a semi-auto espresso machine" → give generic 18g/36g starting point, suggest user confirm basket stamp.
- "Just an electric grinder" → give relative description ("finer than pour-over, coarser than espresso"), suggest user check model.
- Know the model but unsure about profiling → ask "Does your machine panel have a pressure gauge / paddle / curve editor?"

## 6. Pitfall reminders

- ◆ Don't copy a Decent user's dose from a YouTube video — your grinder and beans are different.
- ◆ Don't believe 'bigger basket is better' — match your machine's basket size.
- ◆ Don't change beans + grind + basket + dose simultaneously — change one variable at a time.
- ✓ First shot: standard 9 bar + 18g/36g + 25–30s as baseline; then change one variable for comparison.

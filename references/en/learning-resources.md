# Curated coffee learning resources

> A systematic curation of high-quality coffee learning resources, organized by stage, to help users find a path suited to their level.
>
> **Beginner translation**: "Want to learn more? Here are trustworthy sites and creators by stage, from entry to certification."

## 0. Three-stage growth roadmap

| Stage | Time reference | Goal | Core resources |
|------|---------------|------|----------------|
| Entry | 0–3 months | Master stable output on at least 2 brew methods; build basic flavor vocabulary | Coffee Salon (CN), China Coffee Net (CN), Sweet Maria's |
| Intermediate | 3–12 months | Systematic cupping practice; understand extraction theory; pursue SCA Brewing Foundation | Barista Hustle, Perfect Daily Grind, EHS Academy (CN) |
| Professional | 12 months+ | SCA Sensory Skills all levels, Q-Grader certification, systematic sensory training | WCR Lexicon, SCA courses, Le Nez du Cafe |

## 4. SCA certification system overview

SCA (Specialty Coffee Association) credentials span six modules at three levels (Foundation / Intermediate / Professional):

| Module | Content | Target audience |
|------|------|---------|
| Barista Skills | Espresso extraction, milk drinks, latte art | Bar operators |
| Brewing | Pour-over, Golden Cup theory, extraction science | Enthusiasts going deep on brewing |
| Sensory Skills | Cupping, flavor wheel, sensory training | Anyone wanting better tasting ability |
| Roasting | Roasting principles, curve control | Advanced players wanting to roast |
| Green Coffee | Supply-chain management & green grading | Buying & QC |
| Q Grader (CQI) | Coffee quality grader | Advanced course, aimed at industry pros |
